How We Help

We've been helping our neighbours in Lake Limestone, Old Union, Seale, Olthea, and Marquez since 2014. We provide maintenance contracts, inspections, and required reporting that complies with Bravos River Authority (BRA) requirements and repairs. We help property owners keep aerobic systems in working order and respond to emergency calls on weekends and holidays for alarm lights, buzzers, and toilets that don't flush.

Services We Provide

Septic Pumping

Septic systems need regular maitenance in order to keep working efficiently and to prevent premature part failures. We Perform regular pumping and maintenance to prevent sludge build-up that corrodes your septic system.

Repairs

Aerobic systems have mechanical and electrical parts that can wear down and corrode over time. We perform repairs on these parts to make your broken septic system operational again.

Emergencies

There's nothing worse than spending a weekend at Lake Limestone and having problems with your septic system. We respond to service calls on weekends and holidays to give you the assistance you need.

Maintenance

An aerobic system that isn't operating correctly is a serious health hazard. We inspect and report to the BRA every 4 months that your system is operating correctly. We clean each filter so the pump can work efficiently and last longer. We also correct any small issues found during inspections.

Aerobic System Installations

We can perform installations for all septic types. Call us for a quote on installation for your new septic systems
